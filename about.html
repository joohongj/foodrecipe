USDA Expands Nutrition Research Across the U.S.

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is continuing to invest in nutrition research to better understand how diet affects long-term health. New initiatives focus on improving dietary guidelines and promoting healthier food choices, especially among students and low-income communities.

Researchers are studying eating habits, food accessibility, and the impact of processed foods. These findings are expected to influence future policies and public health programs across the country.

Food and Safety

The food landscape in this country is ever changing. And USDA is involved in managing those changes as related to many areas of food processing and food distribution. From the inspection of domestic product, imports, and exports; conducting risk assessments; and educating the public about the importance of food safety, USDA is there.
